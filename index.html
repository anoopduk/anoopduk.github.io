Hello!

Welcome to my personal homepage! I'm Anoop, a academic and researcher focusing on the fields of computational chemistry and machine learning.

Bio

Currently, I’m a Professor in the School of Digital Sciences at Digital University Kerala. Before this, I had the privilege of working as an Assistant and Associate Professor at the Department of Chemistry, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (2010-2023) . I spent a little over four years in Germany, where I was a Postdoctoral Researcher with Prof. Walther Thiel at the Max Planck Institute für Kohlenforschung in Mülheim an der Ruhr and with Prof. Frank Neese at the University of Bonn.

Research

My research centers on computational chemistry and the application of machine learning to drive scientific discovery. While our work spans various aspects of molecular structures and reaction mechanisms, particularly those of interest to my colleagues and collaborators, our primary focus has been on exploring prebiotic chemistry and developing software tools to model complex atomic clusters and nanoclusters.

Other Details

I’ve been fortunate to teach courses on various topics, including Chemistry, Computational Chemistry, Quantum Chemistry, Healthcare Analytics, Photochemistry, and more.

Alongside teaching, I’m also involved in editorial work. I serve as a Subject Editor/Associate Editor for journals like the Journal of Molecular Graphics and Modelling, the Indian Journal of Chemistry, and Frontiers in Chemistry.
