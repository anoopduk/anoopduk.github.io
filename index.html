Welcome

Welcome to my personal homepage. I am Anoop, a Professor at the School of Digital Sciences, Digital University Kerala, specializing in computational chemistry and machine learning. My work focuses on exploring the complex chemical space of novel molecular structures and reactions, using computational techniques and machine learning.

Bio

My academic career includes serving as an Assistant Professor and Associate Professor at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. I conducted postdoctoral research under the mentorship of Prof. Walther Thiel at the Max-Planck Institut fuer Kohlenforschung and Prof. Frank Neese at University of Bonn, Germany. I earned my PhD in Computational Chemistry from the University of Hyderabad guided by Prof. E. D. Jemmis. My undergraduate and master's degrees were completed at Nirmala College and Mahatma Gandhi University, respectively.
