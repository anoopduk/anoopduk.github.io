Research

My research focuses on computational chemistry and the application of machine learning to scientific discovery. I explore molecular structures and reaction mechanisms, with a primary emphasis on prebiotic chemistry. I also develop software tools for modeling complex atomic clusters and nanoclusters.

Professional Roles

I teach courses in Chemistry, Computational Chemistry, Quantum Chemistry, Photochemistry, and Healthcare Analytics. I also serve as an Associate Editor for Journal of Molecular Graphics and Modelling and subject editor for Indian Journal of Chemistry.

I am a member of professional organizations, including the National Academy of Sciences India, the Chemical Research Society of India, and the World Association of Theoretical and Computational Chemists (WATOC). Additionally, I am a member of Board of Studies in Kannur University and Nirmala College (Autonomous).
